Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

Dermaa ("we", "our") is a skincare tracking app. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and the choices you have. The short version: your data is used only to run the features you see in the app — it is never sold or shared for advertising.

What we collect

  • Account details — your email address and password (stored as a secure hash), used to sign you in.
  • Skin photos — selfies you choose to take for skin analysis. Photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage and analyzed by an AI model through our own server. We never use your photos for anything other than producing your analysis, and they are never sold or used to train models.
  • Skin & lifestyle profile — the answers you give at onboarding (skin type, concerns, diet, sleep, allergies, budget), used to personalize routines, analysis, and recommendations.
  • Daily logs — check-ins (mood, symptoms, triggers), routine completion, meals and meal photos, water and sleep entries. Used to build your dashboard, trends, and personalized AI guidance.
  • Coach conversations — messages you exchange with the AI coach, stored so the conversation continues across sessions.
  • App version & basic diagnostics — which app version you run, so we can support you and fix bugs.

How AI features work

Skin analysis, meal analysis, the coach, and care-plan tips are produced by an AI model. Your photo or relevant data is sent from our server to the AI provider strictly to generate your result, then the result is stored in your account. AI output is general guidance — it is not medical advice, and it never replaces a dermatologist.

Where your data lives

Data is stored with Supabase (our backend provider) with row-level security: every record is tied to your account, and only you can read your own data through the app. AI requests are made from our server — API keys and processing never happen on your device.

Subscriptions

Paid subscriptions are processed by Google Play. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription state. We never see or store your payment card details; we only receive confirmation that a subscription is active.

What we never do

  • Sell your data or share it with advertisers.
  • Use your photos for anything except your own analysis.
  • Access your data beyond what operating the service requires.

Your choices

  • You can use the app's tracking features without ever taking a photo.
  • You can edit or clear your profile details in the app at any time.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us — we will remove it within 30 days.
